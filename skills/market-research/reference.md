# Market research — reference

## Competitor profile template

```markdown
### <Competitor>
- **Positioning:** the one-line promise; who it's for.
- **Target segment:** primary customer / ICP.
- **Pricing & packaging:** tiers, model (seat/usage/flat), notable free tier.
- **Go-to-market:** self-serve / sales-led / channel; key partnerships.
- **Strengths:** what they genuinely do well.
- **Weaknesses / gaps:** where they're vulnerable (evidence, not wishful).
- **Recent moves:** launches, raises, hires, pricing changes (with dates).
- **Signals:** job postings, patents, roadmap hints — what they're *about* to do.
- **Sources:** each claim → source + date + credibility.
```

## Source shortlist (public, lawful)

| Source | Signal |
|--------|--------|
| SEC/company filings, annual reports | Revenue, strategy, risks (their own words) |
| Pricing & product pages | Packaging, positioning, ICP |
| Job postings | Hiring by function/geo → roadmap + expansion |
| Review sites (G2, etc.) | Strengths/weaknesses in customers' words |
| Patents | Technical direction |
| Press / blog / changelog | Recent moves, cadence |
| Analyst reports | Market size + landscape (note the sponsor) |
